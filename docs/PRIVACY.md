# Privacy Policy for HomeMap

**Last Updated: November 14, 2025**

## Overview

HomeMap is a local network tool for managing Fibaro Home Center 3 (HC3) home automation systems. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

HomeMap does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers or third parties.

### What HomeMap Does:

- **Local Network Only**: All communication occurs directly between your iOS device and your Fibaro HC3 controller on your local network
- **No Cloud Services**: No data is sent to cloud services or external servers
- **No Analytics**: We do not collect usage statistics, analytics, or telemetry
- **No Tracking**: We do not track user behavior or activities
- **No Account Required**: No user accounts, registration, or authentication with external services

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

- **Configuration Settings**: Your HC3 IP address, credentials, and connection settings
- **Floor Plans**: Custom floor plan images and layouts you create
- **Device Mappings**: Your preferences for how devices appear on floor plans
- **Custom Icons**: Any custom icons you import for devices
- **Widget Configurations**: Settings for custom UI widgets

All this data remains on your iOS device and is never transmitted externally.

## Third-Party Services

HomeMap does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Security

- All communication with your HC3 controller can use HTTPS for encrypted connections
- Your HC3 credentials are stored securely in your device's local storage
- No data leaves your local network

## Children's Privacy

HomeMap does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for home automation management and is intended for adult users.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app repository and on the App Store listing.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/jangabrielsson/HomeMap/issues

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by removing the app.

---

**Summary**: HomeMap is a privacy-first application. Your home automation data stays in your home, on your network, under your control.
